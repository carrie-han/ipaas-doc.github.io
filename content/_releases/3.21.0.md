## Recipes Stuff
You can find the corresponding API doc [here]({{site.data.tenant.apiBaseUri}}/docs/v2/#update-a-recipe-visibility)). `POST v2/recipes` and `PATCH v2/recipes/:id` do not have `attributes.visibility` anymore.
